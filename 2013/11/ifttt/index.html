But what is IFTTT?

IFTTT is a service that lets you create powerful connections [between your online accounts] with one simple statement: If This Then That (Image Via: IFTTT)

So basically, IFTTT allows you to connect your online services via their API‘s. These connections are called recipes  which are made from the phrase IF THIS THEN THAT.
